Taco

Description/h2>

This slow cooker chicken tacos recipe is easy to make with just 3 ingredients for flavorful, super tender, shredded chicken. Spoon the chicken into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  4. Cook on Low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken with two forks.
